The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permenant sturctures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, a roof dome that can be opened, and a private deck.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a gas fireplace, king-size bed with down quilt, comfy couch, dining table and chairs, mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker. Your luxury camping experience includes a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
